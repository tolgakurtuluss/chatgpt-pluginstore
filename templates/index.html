Frequently Asked Questions ❓

What are ChatGPT Plugins? 🧩

ChatGPT Plugins are third-party add-ons that allow ChatGPT to perform further functions beyond the basic operations and tasks that the platform is equipped to handle. These plugins are designed to be used with ChatGPT and they provide additional functionalities to the language model.

How can I enable plugins into ChatGPT? ⚙️

To enable third-party plugins in ChatGPT, navigate to https://chat.openai.com/ and login. Then, by clicking on the left bottom three dots and select “Settings”. In the settings menu, locate the “Beta Features” option and turn on “Web browsing” and “Plugin” features.

Is there any documentation for ChatGPT Plugins? 📚

Of course! You can access the official documentation on https://platform.openai.com/docs/plugins/introduction

What are the policies for using plugins on ChatGPT? 📜

All usage policies are detailed on the official OpenAI website on https://openai.com/policies/usage-policies#plugin-policies
